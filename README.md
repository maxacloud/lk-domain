## 🌍 About LK Domains

An **LK domain** is the official country-code top-level domain (ccTLD) for Sri Lanka.

It is commonly used for:
- Business websites targeting Sri Lanka  
- Government and institutional platforms  
- Local startups and digital services  
- Agencies and hosting providers  

Reliable automation is essential when you want to register LK domains at scale.
